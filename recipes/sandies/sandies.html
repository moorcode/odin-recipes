Vegan Pecan Sandies

Ingredients

  • flour
  • vegan butter (keep refrigerated)
  • sugar
  • sea salt
  • cinnamon
  • pecans
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine and whisk dry ingredients.
  2. Combine and mix together butter and sugar.
  3. In portions, mix dry ingredients into buttery sugar.
  4. In a skillet, lightly roast crushed pecans.
  5. Crush pec
  6. Let sit 5 minutes.
