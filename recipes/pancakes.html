Vegan Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

Crispy edges...buttery, fluffy goodness...what more do you need?

Maple syurp. And dairy-free vanilla yogurt, if you're me. And some raspberries will go nicely as well. Whatever you decide for toppings, do follow the recipe as written - it was crafted from 3 different recipes to be the best vegan pancake in the galaxy.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • sea salt
  • cinnamon
  • vegan butter
  • plant
  • cashew milk
  • lemon

Steps

Buttermilk

  1. Squeeze lemon juice into 1 cup measure.
  2. Fill measure to 1 cup with milk.
  3. Let sit while working on batter.

Pancake Batter

  1. Combine and whisk dry ingredients.
  2. Melt togeter vegan butter and plant.
  3. Pour melted butter into dry ingredients while whisking, allowing yellow clumps to form. Do this for 3 turns.
  4. Pour buttermillk.
  5. Whisk carefully, folding here and there, until formed into lumpy batter.
  6. Let sit 5 minutes.
