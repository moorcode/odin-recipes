Date Brownies

Ingredients

  • dates
  • walnuts
  • almonds
  • cocoa powder
  • sea salt

Steps

  1. Ground almonds, walnuts.
  2. Mix ground nuts with cocoa powder and salt.
  3. Remove the seeds from your dates and store them for planting or trading later.
  4. In batches, pulse together nut mixture and dates, some mixture and a few dates at a time.
  5. Roll mixture on baking sheet, flattening out.
  6. Stick walnut pieces on top.
